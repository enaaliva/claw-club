# 🐾 Claw Club

> *Some memories deserve more than a folder full of photos.*

Welcome to **Claw Club**  a little corner of the internet I built for the cats who've shaped my life in ways I never expected.

Every page here is dedicated to one of them. Their personalities, their favorite spots to nap, the tiny habits that made them *them*, and the memories I never want to forget.

This isn't just a gallery.

It's a digital scrapbook, a diary, and a place where I can keep their stories alive.

---

## 🌼 What's inside?

 Individual pages for each cat

 A growing collection of photos and little moments

 Stories, memories, and personality notes

 Scrapbook-inspired layouts

 Small interactive details hidden throughout the site

---

## 💻 Built with

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript

No frameworks.

Just curiosity, patience, and way too much time spent nudging elements by a few pixels until they felt right.

---

## 🤍 Why this project exists

I realized that memories fade faster than we'd like.

Photos get buried in galleries.
Videos disappear into old folders.
Little details slowly become harder to remember.

I didn't want that to happen.

Claw Club became my way of collecting those moments in one place a website where every page feels like opening a different chapter.

Maybe it's a little sentimental.

I think that's exactly what makes it worth building.

---

## 🌱 Still growing

Just like life, this website isn't really finished.

There are always more photos to add, more stories to write, and more little interactions waiting to be built.

---
